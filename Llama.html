Llama Trauma

Description

Play at: https://itch.io

Play with a friend, or challenge yourself to help both these llamas to the lake. In this epic saga, two llamas try helping their pal, the fish, to get home to the lake. Join these unliekely friends as they combat unfriendly terrain, and some development bugs and try to get the fish home!

The journey is full of jumping puzzles that will challenge any gamer, and don’t forget that llamas aren’t made for carrying fish! Keep an eye on the fish timer, or else the fish might slip off and gameover.

Thanks for playing! Watch out for that fish timer!

Sound Engineering: Felix Dore
Programmers: Ricardo Liganor, Artem Mikhalitsin

My Role

My role for this game was the co-designer and developper. Given the theme of unlikely friends, we conceptualized the basic gameplay we desired and got to work from there. From the begining I started mocking a prototype level and creating objects in the world to interact with. I moved on then to player controls, and jumping mechanics. Once sorted, I worked on the COOP player controls, and setting up the fish passing scripts. I then worked on the graphics and animations, and tweaking some in game physics engines.
